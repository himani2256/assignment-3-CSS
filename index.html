About Chocolate Ice Cream

Chocolate ice cream originated in the 17th century and remains one of the most popular flavors worldwide. Its rich, creamy taste is a favorite for people of all ages.

Nutritional Information

  • Calories: 210 per serving
  • Fat: 12g
  • Sugar: 18g

Fun Facts

  • Chocolate ice cream was the first flavor created after vanilla.
  • It's often used as a base for other ice cream flavors like rocky road.
